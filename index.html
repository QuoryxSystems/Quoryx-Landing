The Problem

Business groups running multiple entities spend 25+ hours monthly on manual intercompany reconciliation. Transactions recorded in one entity rarely match their counterparts, forcing accountants into endless Excel spreadsheets hunting for discrepancies.

The Solution

Quoryx automates intercompany transaction matching and reconciliation natively within Xero and QuickBooks. Our platform identifies mismatches instantly, posts correcting journals automatically, and produces consolidated financial statements with zero manual intervention.
